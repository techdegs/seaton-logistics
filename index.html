FAQs

Frequently Ask Question

01 What services does Seaton Logistics offer?

Seaton Logistics offers acquisition and shipping services for tools, machinery, and cars from the UK and US to Ghana and other African countries. We provide sourcing, purchasing, and logistics solutions to ensure your items arrive safely and on time.

02 How do I place an order with Seaton Logistics?

To place an order, you can contact us via our website, phone, or email. Provide details about the items you need, and our team will assist you through the entire process, from sourcing to delivery.

03 What types of tools do you offer?

We offer a wide range of tools, including hand tools, power tools, construction tools, and automotive tools. All our tools are sourced from reputable suppliers in the UK and US to ensure high quality and reliability.

04 Can you help me purchase specific machinery?

Yes, we specialize in sourcing and shipping various types of machinery, including industrial, construction, agricultural, and manufacturing equipment. If you have specific requirements, our team can help find the right machinery for your needs.

06 What types of cars can I buy through Seaton Logistics?

We can help you acquire new and used cars, luxury cars, and commercial vehicles from the UK and US. Our team will handle the entire process, from finding the perfect car to ensuring it is shipped safely to your location.

07 How long does it take to receive my items?

The delivery time depends on several factors, including the type of item, its location, and the shipping method. Our team will provide you with an estimated delivery time once your order is confirmed.

08 What are the shipping costs?

Shipping costs vary based on the size, weight, and destination of the items. We provide a detailed quote for shipping costs once we have all the necessary information about your order.

09 Do you offer any guarantees on the items you ship?

We work with trusted suppliers to ensure the quality of the items we source. While we do not offer specific guarantees, we strive to ensure that all items meet high standards of quality and reliability. Any manufacturer warranties or guarantees will be passed on to you.
